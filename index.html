Privacy Policy – Moirai Within

This Privacy Policy explains how the Moirai Within app handles your data. Short version: Moirai Within does not collect, store or share any personal data from your device. All of your content stays on your device and is never sent to any external server by the app.

Data we do not collect

Moirai Within does not:

  • Collect your name, email address or contact details inside the app
  • Collect your tarot readings, journal entries, rituals, or any other in-app content
  • Collect location data
  • Collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Track your behavior across other apps or websites
  • Use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs or tracking tools

The app does not require you to create an account or sign in.

Data stored on your device

All content you create inside Moirai Within — such as tarot spreads, intentions, rituals and journal notes — is stored locally on your device.

This data is under your control:

  • You can delete entries from inside the app where such options exist
  • You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app from your device

Moirai Within does not transmit this local content to any external server operated by the developer.

Purchases

If Moirai Within offers in-app purchases or subscriptions through the App Store, these transactions are processed by Apple. The app itself does not receive your full payment details. Apple may process basic information related to your purchase in accordance with Apple’s own privacy policy and App Store terms.

Support contact

If you contact me by email at arcanadaily@gmail.com , I will receive the information you choose to include in your message (such as your email address and any details you share about an issue).

I will use this information only to:

  • Reply to your question or support request
  • Improve the app based on anonymised feedback

If you want any email you sent (and associated data) to be deleted, you can request this in a follow-up email.

Children’s privacy

Moirai Within is not designed for children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal data from children. Because no data is collected from any user, there are no special tracking or profiling features for children.

Changes to this policy

If this Privacy Policy ever changes (for example, if the app adds optional online features), the text will be updated here and the App Store privacy information will be adjusted accordingly. Any change that involves collecting data will be clearly explained, and you will always be able to choose whether to use new features that require data.
